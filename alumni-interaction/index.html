Students of MJCET create all-terrain vehicle for SAE-BAJA event
The students of Muffakham Jah College of Engineering and Technology (MJCET) has designed and fabricated all-terrain vehicle for SAE-BAJA 2020 competition to be held at Indore in Madhya Pradesh and the team of MJCET would be leaving for the competition on January 15.

The team of 25 mechanical and production engineering students of MJCET has made this vehicle with the cost of Rs. 5 lakh funded by the Sultan-ul-Uloom Education Society. On Friday, the Chairman and the Secretary of Sultan-ul-Uloom Education Society have flagged off and unveiled the vehicle at MJCET. Captain of team cum driver Murtuza Khan informs that the vehicles have been designed by using Briggs, Stratton engine, Fox suspension, special alloy and steel material for roll cage fabrication to enhance the vehicle performance. The net weight of the vehicle is 180 kg and runs at about 60 KMPH at full throttle
